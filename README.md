# hello-world

I am a research scientist with a background in neurophysiology and a strong interest in data science. My work bridges the gap between technology and human cognitive behaviour, with a focus on understanding how data-driven approaches can uncover patterns in mental processes, health, and decision-making. I enjoy translating complex findings into meaningful insights through thoughtful analysis and visualisation.

This project focused on analysing sleep patterns using a real-world dataset to identify trends and factors influencing sleep quality. The goal was to understand the relationships between variables such as sleep duration, heart rate, activity level, and stress.
